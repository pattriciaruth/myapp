Overview

This app helps caf√© or bakery staff keep track of stock levels for hot food, sweets, beverages and other items.

Adding an item

  1. Go to the Manage items page from the top menu.
  2. Fill in the form: Item ID, Name, Category, Quantity, Price, Supplier, Stock Level and any comments.
  3. Click Add Item to save it to the in-memory list.

Editing an item

  1. On the Manage items page, find the item in the table.
  2. Click Edit to load the values back into the form.
  3. Change the values and click Save Changes.

Deleting an item

  1. On the Manage items page, locate the item.
  2. Click Delete and confirm the prompt.

Searching and filtering

  1. Go to the Search & filter page.
  2. Type part of a name, choose a category or tick Popular items only.
  3. The results table updates based on the current filters.

Note: this is a training app. All data is cleared when the browser tab is closed or refreshed.
