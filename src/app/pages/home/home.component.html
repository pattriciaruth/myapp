Welcome to the Café & Bakery Inventory App

This small Angular application helps a café / bakery keep track of hot foods, sweets, beverages and other items. It is designed as a learning example for PROG2005 using TypeScript and Angular.

  • Manage items – add, edit or delete inventory items.
  • Search & filter – quickly find items by name, category or popularity.
  • Privacy & security – explains how the app handles data and validation.
  • Help – short guide on how to use the system.
