Welcome to the Café & Bakery Inventory App

This Angular application helps a café or bakery keep track of hot foods, sweets, beverages and other items. It was built as part of PROG2005 to practice TypeScript and Angular.

  • Manage items – add, edit or delete inventory items.
  • Search & filter – quickly find items by name, category or popularity.
  • Privacy & security – explains how data and validation are handled.
  • Help – a short guide on how to use the system.
